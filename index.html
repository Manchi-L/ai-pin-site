01 — 核心功能 / Core Features

用最自然的方式，
留住每一个好想法

The most natural way to capture what matters.

选中即 Pin

Select to Pin

鼠标划选任意文字，弹出工具栏一键 Pin。支持颜色标记：黄、绿、蓝、粉、紫。

Select any text to reveal the floating toolbar. Pin instantly with one click. Choose from five highlight colors.

统一侧边栏收集

Unified Sidebar

所有平台的 Pin 汇聚到一个侧边栏，按日期分组，实时同步更新。

All pins across every platform collect into a single sidebar. Grouped by date, synced in real time.

搜索 & 来源筛选

Search & Filter by Platform

全文搜索 Pin 内容，或点击平台标签快速筛选来自 Claude、ChatGPT、Kimi 等的内容。

Full-text search across all pins. Click platform tags to filter by Claude, ChatGPT, Kimi, and more.

跳转定位

Jump to Source

点击 Pin 卡片的「跳转」按钮，自动切换标签页并滚动到原文位置，高亮闪烁提示。

Click Jump on any pin card to switch tabs and scroll directly to the original text, with a highlight flash.

勾选导出

Select & Export

点选想要导出的 Pin，支持 TXT / Markdown / JSON 三种格式，满足不同使用场景。

Check the pins you want, then export as TXT, Markdown, or JSON to fit any workflow.

日 / 夜模式

Light & Dark Mode

侧边栏与页面内工具栏同步切换明暗主题，偏好自动记忆，护眼不间断。

The sidebar and in-page toolbar both follow your theme preference, remembered automatically.

隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

不收集任何个人数据

AI Pin 不收集、不存储、不传输任何个人身份信息，包括姓名、邮箱、IP 地址或任何可识别用户身份的数据。

AI Pin does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information, including names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any data that could identify you.

标注内容仅存于本地

你创建的所有 Pin 仅保存在你自己浏览器的本地存储（chrome.storage.local）中，不会上传至任何服务器，不会被开发者访问。卸载扩展后数据随之清除。

All pins you create are saved exclusively in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local). This data never leaves your device, is never uploaded to any server, and is never accessible to the developer. Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data.

不使用第三方服务

扩展不集成任何分析工具、广告网络或第三方 SDK，不向任何外部服务发送请求。

The extension does not integrate any analytics tools, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs. No requests are sent to any external services.

页面内容的访问范围

扩展仅在用户主动划选文字时读取所选内容，用于创建标注。不监控浏览行为，不记录键盘输入，不扫描未选中的页面内容。

The extension only reads text that you explicitly select, solely to create a pin. It does not monitor browsing behavior, log keystrokes, or scan any page content you have not selected.

权限说明

扩展申请的权限（storage、sidePanel、tabs、contextMenus）及主机权限，均仅用于实现标注、收集和跳转定位功能，不用于任何数据采集目的。

The permissions requested (storage, sidePanel, tabs, contextMenus) and host permissions are used solely to enable pinning, collecting, and jump-navigation. They are not used for any data collection purpose.

政策更新

如本政策发生变更，将在此页面更新。

If this policy changes, updates will be reflected on this page.
